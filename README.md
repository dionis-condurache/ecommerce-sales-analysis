# E-commerce Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

## 1. Business Problem
Analyze e-commerce data to identify revenue trends, product performance, and customer behavior in order to provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## 2. Dataset
- Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist)
- Multiple tables: orders, order items, products, customers

---

## 3. Tools Used
- Google BigQuery (SQL)

---

## 4. Data Preparation
- Joined multiple tables to create a unified dataset
- Filtered only delivered orders for accurate revenue analysis
- Extracted date fields for time-based analysis

---

## 5. Key Insights

### Revenue Trends
- Revenue showed consistent growth until May 2018
- Slight decline afterward suggests possible seasonality or demand shift

### Product Performance
- Top categories:
  - Beauty & Health
  - Watches & Gifts
  - Home & Bath

- Revenue is driven by:
  - High-volume low-cost products
  - High-value premium products

### Category Strategy
- Home & Bath → high volume, low price
- Watches & Gifts → lower volume, high price
- Beauty & Health → balanced category

### Customer Behavior
- Majority of customers are one-time buyers (~99k)
- Indicates low customer retention

---

## 6. Recommendations

- Improve customer retention through loyalty programs and follow-ups
- Focus on high-performing categories for targeted marketing
- Promote repeat purchases in volume-driven categories
- Leverage premium categories for high-margin sales

---

## 7. Conclusion
This analysis highlights key revenue drivers and customer behavior patterns, providing actionable insights to improve business performance and long-term growth.
